The Gear

Here you will find the gear we're using while cycling across Canada. When we get back we will add our reviews and let you know if it stood up to the test.

The Bikes

We have made our our first major purchase, our bikes. I'm happy to say when cycling across Canada we will be on Canadian bikes. Both of us will be riding the Brodie Argus bike. The Argus touring bike with a steel frame and disc breaks, a nice sturdy bike with good breaking to help us down the moutains.

The Tent

Our tent will be the roof over our heads for many nights. We have chosen the Hubba Hubba NX tent by MRS. This tent strikes the right balance between space, waterproofing and weight.

The Sleeping Bags

Alpkit offered good value for money and after comparing the down and synthetic version of this sleeping bag and being unable to tell the difference we went for the synthetic at almost half the cost.

The Panniers

We liked the look, the simplicty, the waterproofing and price of these Crosso Panniers. We have 2 for the front and 2 for the back to help balance the weight on the bike.
